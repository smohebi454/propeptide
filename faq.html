Everything you need to know about the Retatrutide 30mg FlexiPen.

Retatrutide is a novel synthetic triple-agonist peptide that simultaneously targets three metabolic receptors: GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1), GIP (glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide), and glucagon receptors. This triple mechanism of action distinguishes it from dual-agonists like tirzepatide and single-agonists like semaglutide, making it a uniquely versatile tool for metabolic research.

The FlexiPen contains 30mg of retatrutide delivered across 300 clicks at 0.1mg per click. The dial can be set up to 60 clicks per administration, providing a maximum single dose of 6mg. Simply dial to your desired number of clicks, attach a sterile needle from the included supply, and administer. The pen mechanism ensures precise, reproducible dosing for research protocols.

Our retatrutide is independently verified at 99.262% purity via HPLC analysis by Janoshik Analytical, a leading third-party peptide testing laboratory. The current batch is RETP002. Full certificates of analysis are available on our Test Results page.
